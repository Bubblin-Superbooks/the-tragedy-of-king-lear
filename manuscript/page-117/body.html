No, you unnatural hags!I will have such revenges on you bothThat all the world shall- I will do such things-What they are yet, I know not; but they shall beThe terrors of the earth! You think I'll weep.No, I'll not weep.I have full cause of weeping, but this heartShall break into a hundred thousand flawsOr ere I'll weep. O fool, I shall go mad!
