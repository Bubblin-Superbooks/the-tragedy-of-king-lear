Scene Iii.

The British Camp, Near Dover

Enter, in conquest, with Drum and Colours, Edmund; Lear and Cordelia as prisoners; Soldiers, Captain

Edm. Some officers take them away. Good guardUntil their greater pleasures first be knownThat are to censure them
