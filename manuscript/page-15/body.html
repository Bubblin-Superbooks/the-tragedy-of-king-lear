Followers Lear. Attend the lords of France and Burgundy, Gloucester

Glou. I shall, my liege.

Exeunt [Gloucester and Edmund]

Lear. Meantime we shall express our darker purpose.Give me the map there. Know we have dividedIn three our kingdom; and 'tis our fast intentTo shake all cares and business from our age,Conferring them on younger strengths while weUnburthen'd crawl toward death. Our son of Cornwall,And you, our no less loving son of Albany,We have this hour a constant will to publishOur daughters' several dowers, that future strifeMay be prevented now.
