Lear. Ha! Goneril with a white beard? They flatter'd me like a
dog,and told me I had white hairs in my beard ere the black oneswere there. To say 'ay' and 'no' to everything I said! 'Ay'
and'no' too was no good divinity. When the rain came to wet meonce, and the wind to make me chatter; when the thunder
wouldnot peace at my bidding; there I found 'em, there I smelt
'emout. Go to, they are not men o' their words! They told me I
waseverything. 'Tis a lie- I am not ague-proof
