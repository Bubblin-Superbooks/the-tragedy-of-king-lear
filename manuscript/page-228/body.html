Exeunt [Lear and Cordelia, guarded]

Edm. Come hither, Captain; hark.Take thou this note [gives a paper]. Go follow them to
prison.One step I have advanc'd thee. If thou dostAs this instructs thee, thou dost make thy wayTo noble fortunes. Know thou this, that menAre as the time is. To be tender-mindedDoes not become a sword. Thy great employmentWill not bear question. Either say thou'lt do't,Or thrive by other means

Capt. I'll do't, my lord

Edm. About it! and write happy when th' hast done.Mark- I say, instantly; and carry it soAs I have set it down

Capt. I cannot draw a cart, nor eat dried oats;If it be man's work, I'll do't.
